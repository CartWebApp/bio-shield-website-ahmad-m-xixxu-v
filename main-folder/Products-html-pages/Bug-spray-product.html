Bio-Shield Bug Spray

Price: $6.99

Description:

Bio-Shield Bug Spray is a powerful and effective solution for eliminating a wide range of insects, including mosquitoes, flies, ants, and more. This spray is formulated with advanced ingredients that target and kill bugs on contact, providing quick relief from annoying pests.

The bug spray is easy to use and can be applied directly to areas where insects are present, such as outdoor patios, gardens, and indoor spaces. It is designed to provide long-lasting protection, helping to keep your environment bug-free for extended periods.

To use the bug spray simply shake the can well before use. Hold the can upright and spray from a distance of about 18-24 inches onto surfaces where bugs are commonly found. Avoid spraying directly on food, pets, or humans. For best results, reapply as needed or as directed on the product label.
