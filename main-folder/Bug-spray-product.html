Bio-Shield Bug Spray

Product Description

Bio-Shield Bug Spray is a powerful and effective solution for eliminating a wide range of insects, including mosquitoes, flies, ants, and more. This spray is formulated with advanced ingredients that target and kill bugs on contact, providing quick relief from annoying pests.

More Information To use the bug spray simply shake the can well before use. Hold the can upright and spray from a distance of about 18-24 inches onto surfaces where bugs are commonly found. Avoid spraying directly on food, pets, or humans. For best results, reapply as needed or as directed on the product label. Although bug spray looks similar to our bug repellent the difference is Bug repellents sprays chemicals to insects dislike making them less likely to come near while our bug spray uses harmful chemicals for the bug to kill it.
