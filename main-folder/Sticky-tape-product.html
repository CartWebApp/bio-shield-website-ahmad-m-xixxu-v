Bio-Shield Sticky Tape

Product Description

Bio-Shield Sticky Tape is a highly effective solution for capturing and controlling flying insects such as flies, mosquitoes, and gnats. This sticky tape is coated with a strong adhesive that traps insects upon contact, preventing them from escaping. Users don't have to worry about it sticking together as the adhesive isn't strong enough to bond unless firmly pressed together. The sticky tape is also double-sided for placements on ceilings and floors.
